## 2026

**Capital Markets LLM Reliability Score (CM-LRS): From Plausible to Bankable.** P. Ahuja. *arXiv preprint*, 2026. arXiv link coming soon · [code & data](https://github.com/dsauce/cm-lrs) · [verification script](https://github.com/dsauce/cm-lrs/blob/main/eval/verify_numbers.py)

A 7-dimension reliability metric for LLM outputs in capital-markets workflows. Demonstrated on five workflows across four models scored by four independent LLM judges from three model families. Includes a deterministic verification script (95 hardcoded checks; all pass).
